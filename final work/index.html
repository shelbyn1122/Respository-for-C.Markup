About Haarlem

Who doesn't dream about touring Amsterdam one day? Amsterdam is a wonderful city, full of life, where you can party all day and all night. However, most clever tourists will soon realize that Amsterdam is very crowded, and Amsterdam has lost some of it's character to it's masssive amount of tourism every year. So if you really want to explore the Netherland, but don't want to be caught in a tourist trap, where can you go?

Haarlem is a lovely city that lies only 15 minutes south of Amsterdam. Haarlem is special because it has a similar feel as Amsterdam, but hasn't yet lost it's authenticity to loads of tourists. In Haarlem you can find all the Dutch culture you are looking for without giving into the tourist trap prices, and watch Dutch people in their natural habitat, just watch out for the cycylists as they will run you over.

What's in Haarlem for you?

Explore the city

Thinking about joining a sports club in Haarlem but wonder what else there is to do here? Haarlem has many different shops, restaurants and activities to offer. If would like to spend a day out shopping, you can visit Haarlem city centrum, where you can find many shops both local and chains, such as H&M, Apple, Zara, Callaysta Jewelery, and many more? Like something a bit more uncommon? Haarlem also has great vintage shops, such as Vintage Island. If you would rather go to restaurant, Haarlem also has a lot in store for you. You can go to Sumo Sushi, and fill your plate at their all you can eat buffet. If you prefer Italian food, try Back to Basics cucina italiana, where pizzas are made in wood oven just like traditional Italian style pizzas. Are you looking for some special bier you can only get in the Netherlands or Belgium? Haarlem also has plenty of café’s that serve special biers, like Café Koops.

If you’re looking to purchase local goods, the market in Haarlem is great option for you. Held on Mondays and Saturdays in Haarlem, local vendors sell their items in the Grote Market. Visit to buy some fresh cheese, stroopwafels, fresh meats, and clothing items for a fair price. Looking for more recreational activities? You can visit Escape Haarlem to challenge yourself and friends to an escape room adventure, with an history background. Are you interested in escaping reality? There is also a Virtual Reality center in Haarlem, called VRcafe. If you aren’t as into the tech games, the Boardroom in Haarlem is another café that features many board games you can play with friends while enjoying a drink or small bite. If nothing else appeals, you can always relax and catch a film at the Pathe, as many films are offered in English in the Netherlands.
